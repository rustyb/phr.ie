At PHR we track all planning applications across Ireland. We provide some specialist services tracking the energy sector, specifically solar farms. We enhance baseline planning information with energy related data, grid connection information and planing appeals.

Currently there are over 300 planning applications on file for utility scale solar farms in Ireland. Below you can see a breakdown of the top 15 applicants as ordered by planned maximum export capacity.

It's interesting to note that the majority of solar farm applicants that receive planning consent have also secured grid connection offer with either ESB or EirGrid. Looking at the expiry date of these applications there is clearly two intense periods of construction, the first in the next 3 years and the second beyond 2026.

We provide a weekly planning status update for the solar sector in Ireland. On a monthly basis we also provide a complete database of planning applications matched to grid connection offers. Currently this is confined to solar and energy storage applications.
